About Kea Interiors

Spaces people love coming back to

Kea Interiors was founded on a simple belief — a home should feel as good as it looks. We pair architectural precision with warm, livable materiality, working closely with luxury homeowners and growing families to design spaces that hold up to everyday life and still feel considered in every corner.

Design environments that elevate how our clients live, work, and gather.

Our Process

From first conversation to final reveal

01

Discovery Consultation

We learn how you live, what frustrates you about your current space, and where you want to end up — in person or virtually.

02

Concept & Space Plan

Moodboards, material palettes, and layout options tailored to your brief and budget, refined together until it feels right.

03

Sourcing & Craft

We source premium materials and coordinate trusted craftspeople for cabinetry, furniture, and finishes.

04

Install & Reveal

Full installation, styling, and a final walkthrough — handing you a space that's ready to live in from day one.
